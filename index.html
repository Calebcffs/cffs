Biography

Edwin Hart is a British writer of non-fiction whose work blends meticulous research with clear, humane storytelling. He trained as a marine biologist before turning to journalism and now writes about the edges where science, history, and everyday lives meet.

His essays have appeared in major magazines and his books have been adopted on courses in history of science and environmental policy. He has reported from coastal communities around the world and is currently developing a series on freshwater restoration in European cities.
